Cold Brew - The best brew no matter the season

Cold brew is my favorite way to drink coffee. It's chilled over ice, refreshing, and less acidic than coffee brewed with hot water.

To make cold brew, you only need a few things: the coffee, a jar or pitcher, a strainer and a refrigerator. Put your ground coffee into the jar, fill the jar with water, and allow it to set for 12-24 hours in the fridge. Once the time has passed, simply strain out the grounds and pour yourself a cold glass.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground coffee
  • 1300ml cold water

Steps

  1. Pour your coffee grounds into your jar or pitcher.
  2. Fill the container with water and give it a good shake.
  3. Place your container in the fridge and wait 12-24 hours.
  4. Remove the lid from your container and strain the coffee grounds out.
  5. Serve over ice and add milk if you wish.
